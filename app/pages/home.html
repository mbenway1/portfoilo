About

Michael Benway is a small town guy, raised on a farm in Central Illinois. He is not afraid to get his hands dirty and is always ready to jump into programming. Michael is down for adventure and to try new things. He was trained at Parkland College earning an A.A.S. in Digital Media; work experience includes FASS, Inc., 2wav, and was and intern at Precision Graphics—totaling to four years.

Michael’s passion is photography, reveling in pinhole photography, due to its challenging nature. Pinhole leads back to the day of film; when the outcome was circumventive. Panoramic photography relishes in the adventurous nature of Michael. When he is not behind “screens”, he can be found rock climbing, working on the farm, and exploring cultures. Michael embraces “the challenge” and is willing to go through extreme lengths to get the perfect experience
